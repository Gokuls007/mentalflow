# Marketing Playbook: Launching MentalFlow

This document outlines the marketing and user acquisition strategy for the initial 12 months post-launch.

## 🧭 Messaging Pillars
- **Personalized Path**: "Therapy that adapts to you, not the other way around."
- **Clinical Integrity**: "Evidence-based Behavioral Activation, powered by AI."
- **Gamified Growth**: "Transform your mental health journey into meaningful progress."

---

## 🚀 Activation Phases

### Phase 1: Awareness (Months 1-2)
- **Influencer Strategy**: Partner with 10 Micro-influencers in the Wellness/Mental Health space for "Mood Journey" stories.
- **PR Outreach**: Targeting health-tech publications (e.g., MedCity News, MobiHealth) focusing on the "AI + BAT" angle.
- **Content**: Launch the "Vibrant Mind" blog with 2 SEO-optimized posts per week.

### Phase 2: User Acquisition (Months 3-6)
- **Paid Ads**: Google Ads (Targeting: "Anxiety help", "Depression treatment symptoms").
- **Clinician Referral**: Direct outreach to 500 private practices offering a free "Therapist Dashboard" trial.
- **Social**: Instagram/TikTok ads focusing on the "Liquid UI" aesthetic and game mechanics.

### Phase 3: Retention & Scale (Months 7-12)
- **Referral Loops**: "Invite a friend for Bonus XP" program.
- **Enterprise Sales**: B2B outreach to HR departments for employee wellness benefits.
- **Partnerships**: Collaborative content with organizations like NAMI or Mental Health America.

---

## 📊 Acquisition KPIs
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Target < $12.00.
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Target > $120.00.
- **Monthly Active Growth**: Target 15% WoW.

## 💰 Budget Allocation (Seed Phase)
- **40%**: Paid Social Advertising.
- **30%**: Content Creation & PR.
- **20%**: Clinical Outreach.
- **10%**: SEO & Tools.
